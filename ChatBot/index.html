Welcome to Bryan's Joke Bot

The chat bot can interact with greetings such as hello and goodbye as well as tell jokes and react to your response to the joke.

Try it out below!

Steps to interact

  1. Say Hello
  2. Ask the bot to tell you a joke
  3. respond with "lol" or "that was funny"
  4. The bot will react with a gif image
  5. Or if the joke wasn't funny say something like "that was bad"
  6. Say goodbye
